# Phase 8 — Step 8.4: Bank portal polish (decision reflected in queue, portfolio status thresholds)

## Objective

Fix the two bank-portal defects from the QA review: **UI-05** (an approve/decline decision is not reflected
in the applicant queue during the session) and **UI-06** (the Portfolio "Healthy" status only appears at a
perfect health score of 100). Reference: [`docs/QA_E2E_FINDINGS.md`](../../docs/QA_E2E_FINDINGS.md) UI-05, UI-06.

## Dependencies

- **Requires:** Bank Applications pipeline (Phase 6.3), Portfolio screen (Phase 6.4), bank endpoints
  (Phase 6.2: applicants, report, decision, portfolio). `frontend/lib/features/bank/**`,
  `backend/.../bank/BankService.java` + `UnderwritingService.java`.

## Tasks

Complete in order:

1. **UI-05 — Reflect the decision in the applicant queue.**
    - After a successful `decision` (Approve/Decline), update the selected applicant's list item so its status
      is visible without a manual reload: show a decided status chip (Approved/Declined) and/or move it out of
      the "Pending review" group. Prefer updating local state from the decision response; refetch the queue if
      simpler and still snappy.
    - Confirm the decision already persists server-side (the Portfolio picks up approved facilities today) —
      this step is about the **queue view** staying in sync, not about persistence.

2. **UI-06 — Review Portfolio health-status thresholds.**
    - Today facilities at health 98–99 show "Watch" and only 100 shows "Healthy" — the Healthy band looks
      unintentionally narrow. Align the status bands with the intended risk policy (e.g. Healthy ≥ 90,
      Watch 70–89, At-risk < 70 — confirm exact cutoffs with product / DESIGN §5.5). Apply consistently
      wherever the status is derived (backend if computed there, else the Portfolio widget).
    - Keep the health **scores** themselves unchanged — only the status-band mapping is adjusted.

3. **Tests.**
    - Applications: after a decision, the queue item reflects the new status (widget/state test).
    - Portfolio: a facility at the chosen "Healthy" cutoff maps to "Healthy" and boundaries map as intended
      (unit test on the band mapping).

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] After Approve/Decline, the applicant's queue entry shows its decided status (or leaves "Pending review")
      without a manual page reload.
- [ ] Portfolio status bands match the agreed risk policy (a healthy facility below 100 no longer shows
      "Watch"); boundaries are covered by a test.
- [ ] Health scores are unchanged; only status labels are affected.
- [ ] Existing bank widget/service tests still pass; new tests pass.
